Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your data is collected, used, and protected when using our app (uJournal). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to keeping your personal information secure and private.

Information We Collect

  • Name: Used to personalize your journaling experience.
  • Email Address: Used for account creation and login.
  • Profile Photo (optional): Used for your personal profile only, not required.
  • Journal Entries: Daily logs written by you and stored securely for your own use.

How We Use Your Information

All information is used solely to provide and improve your experience within the App. We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to any third parties. Your journal entries and profile information are private and visible only to you.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is accessible only through your authenticated account. We use secure storage services (e.g., Firebase) to protect your information.

Advertising and Profit

This app is a non-commercial project. We do not run advertisements or monetize user data in any way.

Third-Party Services

We may use Firebase services (e.g., Authentication, Firestore, Storage), which have their own privacy policies. You can read more here: Firebase Privacy Policy.

Your Rights and Steps to Delete Account

You may contact us at any time to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. You may also delete all your information within the application by logging in and pressing the back button in the home page. Then you will be able to delete your account and any associated data.
